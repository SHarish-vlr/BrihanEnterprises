OUR HISTORY

1992-93
Foundation Launch

Origins: Our Journey Begins

In 1992-93, a vision took root in a small Chembur (Mumbai) office. We started as a recruitment firm with a single staff member, driven by a powerful purpose: to create transformative opportunities for job seekers and to provide businesses with exceptional talent. From those humble beginnings, our commitment to excellence and impact has remained unwavering.

1995
Community Focus

Empowering Communities

Driven by a commitment to community empowerment, our founders launched an NBFC in the mid-1990s, providing affordable loans to small businesses and individuals. This initiative aimed to bridge the financial gap and fuel entrepreneurial growth. However, regulatory changes in 1999 necessitated the cessation of this business line.

1996
Global Synapse

Bridging Global Markets

Driven by a vision to introduce diverse global products to the Indian market, our founders established a marketing and trading venture in the mid-1990s. This initiative aimed to expand access to quality goods and foster international trade. As the business flourished, it was strategically spun off as an independent entity.

1998
Global Horizons

Our International Recruitment Launch

As the late 1990s approached, the demand for specialized Indian talent began to extend beyond national borders. Industries like Insurance and Manufacturing sought skilled professionals in international markets. Recognizing this emerging trend, we embarked on our first international venture, successfully placing talent with the Kuwait Insurance Company in Kuwait. This marked the beginning of our global expansion, connecting Indian expertise with opportunities worldwide.

1999
Meeting the Tech Talent Surge

Launching Our IT Division

By the mid-1990s, the burgeoning IT sector presented a significant opportunity. As companies like Aptech, NIIT, and Silverline Technologies experienced rapid growth, the demand for skilled IT professionals surged. This trend intensified dramatically with the approaching Y2K deadline, leading to a quadrupling of demand by 1999. Recognizing this pivotal moment, we established a dedicated IT recruitment team, solidifying our position as a key player in the evolving tech talent landscape.

2005
Strategic Advisory

Launching Our Consulting Division

In the mid-2000s, recognizing the growing need for specialized advisory services, we expanded our capabilities by establishing a dedicated consulting division. This strategic move aimed to provide enterprises with high-quality consulting, focusing on our core areas of expertise: Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance (BFSI), Communication, Media, and Entertainment (CME), and Technology.

Today

The group now operates under the name of Brihan Enterprises Private Limited. With interests in Recruitment & Staffing, Management Consulting, Technology & IT Services.
